Decolonizing mathematics education by disrupting power through the 4 Rs of Indigenous research

Thesis

Many mathematics teachers in Canada are searching for ways to decolonize their teaching practice and include Indigenous epistemologies and pedagogies in their classroom. Unfortunately, these same mathematics teachers struggle to find practical ways to do this decolonizing work that do not reduce complex Indigenous cultural phenomena to simplistic, and often tokenistic, textbook questions.

Maegan Giroux

Maegan Giroux (she/her) is a passionate instructional coach, dedicated to empowering educators across all levels to excel in their teaching practice. With a specialized focus on literacy and numeracy, she walks alongside teachers as they implement dynamic initiatives to enhance student learning. With a rich background spanning from Kindergarten to grade 12, Maegan brings a wealth of experience to her current roles, with a current focus on her role as a grade 6/7 and Kindergarten teacher.
